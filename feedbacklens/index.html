How it works



Signup with your basic information


Simply add the plug-in provided to you by us on the page you want to gather feedback


Capture and analyze feedback received using tools from feedbacklens

Simplicity is one of the main principles of feedbacklens. We are committed to make your experience as simple as we can and will do everything possible to quickly set you up so that you start get valuable insights of your customers. Following are few details on how it all works

  • As soon as you make up your mind to use feedbacklens as a tool to understand customer insights, please click here to signup . All you have to do is provide some basic information about you to signup;

  • On completion of your signup, you will receive an email about plug-in installation that needs to be integrated with your website page. To get more specific information please do the same on each page that you are willing to collect feedback on.

  • Once you integrate the plug-in you are all set to get the live feedback from your customers, customer will see a feedback icon on left hand side of your home page (or any page per your setup); providing feedback is then just a click away.

  • Now, based on your subscription, you can visit your dashboard panel or simple use our mobile app to monitor the feedback received. It's very easy to access your dashboard panel with credentials you are provided after successful signup. There are various categories which help you to analyse customer insights.

  • You can also download feedback report based on ratings, categories, dates which is available in csv or pdf format .

We take data security very seriously and will be kept confidential, no data will be shared without your prior approval . Please contact us anytime at admin@feedbacklens.com if you need specific information .

About Us

Our Story

UrbanBonds was born due to a fundamental need to automate & streamline the time consuming, effort intensive and unstructured operations of housing societies and residential communities. We took up the challenge of driving societies to 100% automation without any dependency on a substitute software/ system and are immensely happy to see the dream come true.

Our Expertise

With a penchant to simply and resolve intricate problems end to end, Team UrbanBonds is committed to excellence in quality and service till the last smile. We strongly believe that any systemic automation and refinement is only possible when the entire set of processes followed by an organization are perfectly mapped into a scalable and always "ON" system.

Our Approach

At UrbanBonds, we drive immense pleasure by having process excellence. With that goal in mind, our approach is always towards simplification, automation providing tangible benefits in terms of costs, time and value offering.

Why UrbanBonds?

All the time intensive, tedious and unstructured operations of your Society are magically and extraordinarily transformed by our one-stop solution, that is available on the device of your choice, at any time. UrbanBonds provides real time reports and analytics on your data for you to stay abreast with your Society. With the right checks and balances in place, you can relax and reclaim your peace of mind.
